About Accreditation

Accreditation is a vital process that ensures educational institutions meet and maintain high standards of quality education. It involves a comprehensive evaluation of an institution's facilities, faculty, curriculum, and overall educational effectiveness.

The accreditation process at PUP Taguig follows a systematic approach through different levels, each representing increased excellence and quality in education delivery. This process helps us continuously improve our academic programs and institutional effectiveness.

Through AccrediTrack, we streamline the documentation and monitoring of our accreditation journey, ensuring transparency and efficiency in the process.

Levels in Accreditation

Level I

Initial compliance – Areas 1–10

Level II

Continuous improvement & quality implementation

Level III

Mature systems, research, and extension excellence

Level IV

Sustained excellence and international recognition
