Coordinator Guide — Accreditation Support

As Coordinator you guide your program through accreditation readiness. Below are compact, practical steps and best practices to manage reviews and assignments efficiently.

Message from the Director

Thank you for leading this effort. Ensure timely reviews, clear assignments, and steady communication with faculty and offices. Your work keeps our programs prepared and credible.

Coordinator Responsibilities

  • Check submissions for completeness and standards.
  • Assign reviewers and set internal deadlines.
  • Obtain official records from admin units when needed.
  • Track dashboard progress and follow up on delays.

How to Review & Assign (Quick Steps)

  1. Open "Review & Assign" to view pending items by program.
  2. Verify naming, versions, and required attachments.
  3. Assign reviewers, add clear instructions, and set deadlines.
  4. Mark items "Verified" or "Needs Revision" with feedback.

Best Practices & Support

  • Give specific, actionable feedback.
  • Use short checklists per criterion for consistency.
  • Contact the Director: director@pup.edu.
  • For technical help: it-support@pup.edu.
