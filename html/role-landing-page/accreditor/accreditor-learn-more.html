AACCUPP Accreditors — Accreditation Support

As AACCUPP accreditors you provide independent evaluations of program submissions. Below are compact, practical steps and best practices to help you perform reviews and evaluations efficiently.

Message from the Director

Thank you for leading this effort. Ensure timely reviews, clear assignments, and steady communication with faculty and offices. Your work keeps our programs prepared and credible.

Accreditor / Reviewer Responsibilities

  • Check submissions for completeness and conformity with criteria.
  • Evaluate evidence and provide clear, actionable findings.
  • Request official records or clarifications when needed.
  • Document recommendations and follow-up items clearly.

Accreditor — evaluate evidence submissions

  1. Open "Evaluate" to access evidence submissions assigned to you.
  2. Review evidence against the accreditation criteria and note any gaps.
  3. Provide clear findings, recommendations, and any required follow-ups.
  4. Submit your evaluation and final comments through the system.

Best Practices & Support

  • Give specific, actionable feedback.
  • Use short checklists per criterion for consistency.
  • Contact the Director: director@pup.edu.
  • For technical help: it-support@pup.edu.
