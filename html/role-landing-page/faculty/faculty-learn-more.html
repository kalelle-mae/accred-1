About Accreditation — Faculty Guide

Accreditation is a voluntary peer-review process that ensures programs meet quality standards. Your contributions as faculty—submitting documents, participating in assessments, and implementing recommendations—directly support program improvement and recognition. The sections below explain what is expected and how to prepare accurate, well‑organized submissions.

Message from the Program Coordinator

Thank you for your continued support and dedication. Please prioritize accurate and timely submissions. When uploading evidence, follow the checklist and naming conventions so reviewers can quickly locate and verify the required materials. If something is unclear, ask your coordinator early—it's easier to correct issues before formal review.

Faculty Responsibilities

  • Prepare and upload required documents on time.
  • Follow naming conventions and include metadata (author, date, version).
  • Respond to revision requests from your coordinator or reviewers.
  • Provide supporting context in file descriptions where useful.
  • Attend briefings or meetings requested during active accreditation rounds.

How to Prepare Submissions (Step-by-Step)

  1. Review the checklist for the specific criterion before preparing documents.
  2. Create or export files in accepted formats (PDF preferred for final evidence).
  3. Name files using the convention below and include a short description in the upload form.
  4. Upload to "View Submissions" and select the correct program and criterion tags.
  5. Notify your coordinator via the messaging tool if you expect delays or need assistance.

Naming Conventions & Versioning

Use a consistent filename pattern to help reviewers locate items quickly. For full conventions and examples, check here.

Common Issues & Troubleshooting

  • Missing attachments — confirm all required appendices are included.
  • Poor scan quality — use high-resolution scans or export to PDF from original file.
  • Incorrect tagging — double-check program/criterion selection during upload.
  • Access problems — contact IT support with a screenshot and the file name.

Contact & Support

For coordination and content questions, contact your Program Coordinator: coordinator@pup.edu.
For procedural questions, email the Office of the Director: director@pup.edu.
For technical issues with uploads or access, contact IT support at it-support@pup.edu.
