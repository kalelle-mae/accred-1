AccrediTrack: Centralized Accreditation
Document Management System of PUP Taguig

AccrediTrack is a Centralized Accreditation Management System engineered to transform how educational institutions, specifically beginning with PUP Taguig, achieve and maintain academic compliance.

We were founded on the principle of solving the widespread challenge of manual, fragmented accreditation processes. In the past, managing the vast volume of documents and tracking compliance progress meant facing inefficiencies, data inconsistencies, and the persistent risk of data loss. AccrediTrack was created to eliminate these operational barriers.

Our Solution

AccrediTrack provides a single, secure, and intelligent platform for all accreditation activities. We move the process beyond traditional file systems and into a modern, streamlined digital workflow.

Key Features include:


Centralized Repository

A secure, cloud-based hub for all evidence files, structured logically by Areas, Parameters, and Criteria.

Workflow & Task Management

Program Heads and Coordinators can assign requirements to faculty, set deadlines, and track real-time submission progress.

Compliance Monitoring

Dashboards offer readiness scores, heatmaps, and analytics, providing clear visibility for timely decision-making.

Security & Audit

Built with enterprise-level controls, including AES-256 Encryption, OAuth 2.0, and comprehensive audit logs.
